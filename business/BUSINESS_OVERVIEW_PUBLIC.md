# MwangazaMail — Business Overview
> Empowering Governance Through Citizen Intelligence

---

## 1. What We Do

MwangazaMail is a **governance intelligence platform** that enables citizens in the Democratic Republic of Congo to report institutional misconduct through the communication channel they already use every day — without installing an app, without registering, and without barriers.

Every report is captured, structured, and made available to institutions and organisations that subscribe to our platform for real-time visibility into what is happening across their sectors and cities.

We turn citizen voice into actionable governance data.

---

## 2. The Problem

Corruption, abuse of authority, and administrative misconduct are daily realities for millions of Congolese citizens. Yet there is no structured, accessible, real-time system that:

- Lets citizens report safely and easily
- Gives institutions visibility into what is happening
- Provides analysts and NGOs with reliable, queryable data
- Works on the phones and networks people actually have

The result: incidents go unreported, patterns go undetected, and accountability remains weak.

---

## 3. Our Solution

MwangazaMail provides a three-layer ecosystem:

### For Citizens
A familiar, accessible way to report incidents without friction. No account. No download. No technical knowledge required. Citizens report in French, Lingala, or Swahili.

### For Institutions and Subscribers
A secure online dashboard giving real-time visibility into reported incidents filtered by city, category, and type. Subscribers can review, track, and update incident status — and query trends using our built-in AI analysis tool.

### For Data & Accountability
A structured intelligence layer that turns raw reports into analysable, audited data — enabling pattern detection, policy-making, and public accountability at scale.

---

## 4. Who We Serve

- **Government institutions**: municipal authorities, police, customs, tax offices, tribunals
- **International NGOs**: anti-corruption organisations, humanitarian and governance programmes
- **Journalists and researchers**: structured data access to support investigative work
- **Private sector compliance teams**: companies operating in high-risk regulatory environments

---

## 5. Business Model

Institutions and organisations subscribe to the platform to access filtered, real-time incident intelligence.

Three subscription tiers are available — from an entry-level pilot access to a full enterprise tier that includes unlimited users and AI-powered analysis. Subscriptions are monthly.

Revenue potential per subscriber ranges from a few hundred to several thousand dollars per month. The platform reaches profitability with a small number of active subscribers.

---

## 6. Market Opportunity

- DRC urban population (cities covered): 15+ million
- Key cities targeted: Kinshasa, Lubumbashi, Goma, Bukavu, Matadi, Kisangani
- Potential subscriber organisations in DRC alone: 200+
- Regional expansion: Congo-Brazzaville, Burundi, Rwanda represent similar demand

The governance technology space in Francophone Africa is largely unserved by structured SaaS products. MwangazaMail is positioned to define the category.
